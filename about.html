Who We Are

Approaching the commercial cleaning business Gleefully & professionally is our key mission to perfection in cleaning & hygiene solutions.

Our mission, that of “approaching cleaning & hygiene solutions gleefully” is a practice that we apply in all our assignments with special emphasize on client care and satisfaction.
- Glee Hygiene Solutions Ltd represents comprehensively, the “fine art” of general provision of professional commercial cleaning services and hygiene solutions.

Glee Hygiene Solutions Ltd brings you years of professional experience in general in and out door cleaning & hygiene solutions undertakings. Based in Nairobi, Glee boosts a team of;

1. Administration personnel who timely submit efficiently and effectively to clients enquiries.
2. Cleaning staffs who understand the precision in providing cleanliness to the tiniest detail.
3. Field supervisory team who provide regular check and back up to all contract staffing.

At Glee we submit to the highest level of excellence in providing hygiene solutions to commercial buildings, shopping centers and malls, office premises, factories, hospitals & medical Centres, banks & financial Institutions, schools, learning institutions & colleges, and residential premises.
Our commitment to quality extends to every facet of the services offered. We use materials, detergents and equipment, which meet our high standards in our cleaning & hygiene services and are environmental friendly whilst ensuring that such materials surpass the required test in an industry where chemical provisions can’t be destructive to our clients properties.

Likewise, we have over the years adapted methodologies of cleaning geared for safety, reliability, and property care. Our modern cleaning equipment and machines, are always guaranteed to be in top condition. In addition to regular re-training, our personnel receive a minimum of 3 months specialized training in cleaning and are fully informed regarding different types of material and detergents used in cleaning and thus the ability to apply the proper maintenance and procedures. Personnel training is an ongoing tradition at Glee in order to stay updated with the ever changing undertakings in this field.

Each property under our care receives dedicated attention of a supervisor. With years of experience in property care maintenance, these supervisors ensure a constant high level of staff performance.
