PERSONNEL

Glee Hygiene Solutions Ltd ability to provide consistent high quality services is made possible by our deviation from traditional form of cleaning executions whereby our job supervision is overseen by various levels of management.

At Glee Hygiene Solutions we have pioneered a program whereby our staff monitors our clients premises together and guarantee that the customized cleaning schedule will be followed in an efficient, effective and professional manner. We believe that it is not the quantity of supervision that matters but the quality that counts and the skills application ensures that our services will meet clients expectation.

We understand that personnel selection , instruction and evaluation must be conducted accordingly in order to accomplish customers cleaning requirements. Taking into great consideration the requirement of your business nature, our selection of personnel entails a personal interview followed by thorough reference check. Our instruction to the personnel involves combination of method of work, which includes hand-on demonstration and printed instruction. This is to ensure that our crew will be able to execute the task given accurately and confidently.
